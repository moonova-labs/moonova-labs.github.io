Privacy Policy

Budget: Money Tracker

Effective date: July 15, 2026

Budget: Money Tracker ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the App.

By installing or using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

Budget: Money Tracker is designed to help you record and organize your personal finances. Most of the information used by the App is entered directly by you.

This information may include:

  • Income records
  • Expense records
  • Transaction amounts
  • Categories
  • Dates and times
  • Notes you choose to add
  • Budget information

This financial information is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers unless a future feature explicitly states otherwise.

Local Data Storage

Budget: Money Tracker primarily operates offline. Your financial records, transaction history, budgets, and categories are stored on your device.

We do not maintain a cloud database containing your personal financial records.

If you uninstall the App or reset your device, locally stored information may be permanently lost unless you have created your own backup.

Analytics and Diagnostics

To improve the stability and quality of the App, anonymous technical information may be collected by third-party services.

These services may collect information such as:

  • Device model
  • Operating system version
  • Application version
  • Crash reports
  • Diagnostic information
  • Anonymous usage statistics

This information does not include the financial transactions, income, expenses, or notes that you record inside the App.

Third-Party Services

The App may use trusted third-party services to provide analytics, crash reporting, advertising, or essential platform functionality.

These services operate under their own privacy policies.

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics

Advertisements

The free version of the App may display advertisements served by third-party advertising providers such as Google AdMob.

Advertising partners may collect certain device identifiers or advertising identifiers in accordance with their own privacy policies in order to display relevant advertisements and measure advertising performance.

Internet Usage

Most budgeting and expense tracking features work without an internet connection.

Internet access may be required for advertisements, analytics, application updates, or other optional online services.

Cookies

The App does not directly use cookies.

However, integrated third-party SDKs may use technologies similar to cookies or device identifiers for analytics or advertising purposes.

Data Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect information handled by the App.

Since your financial records are stored on your own device, protecting access to your device is also your responsibility. We recommend enabling your device's screen lock or biometric authentication where available.

Children's Privacy

Budget: Money Tracker is not specifically directed toward children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.

In-App Purchases

Some versions of the App may offer optional premium features through Google Play.

Payments

  • Payments are securely processed by Google Play.
  • We do not receive or store your payment card information.

Subscriptions

  • Subscriptions automatically renew unless canceled before the renewal date.
  • You can manage or cancel subscriptions through your Google Play account.

Refunds

  • Refund requests are handled according to Google Play's refund policies and applicable laws.

Your Choices

You may stop using the App at any time by uninstalling it from your device.

Because your financial records are stored locally, uninstalling the App may permanently delete your data unless you have backed it up.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect improvements to the App or changes in applicable laws.
