Privacy Policy

WiFi Manager & Speed Test

Effective date: July 15, 2026

WiFi Manager & Speed Test ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected, how it is used, and how it is protected when you use the App.

By downloading or using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

Most features of the App work directly on your device. We do not collect or store the information displayed by the App, such as your WiFi network information, storage statistics, installed applications, battery information, or device details.

Depending on the features you use, the App may temporarily access information including:

  • Current WiFi connection information
  • Available WiFi network list
  • Internet speed test results
  • Device storage statistics
  • Application list (when you use the App List feature)
  • Battery information
  • Device information

This information is processed locally on your device to provide the requested functionality and is not transmitted to our servers.

Permissions

The App may request certain Android permissions in order to provide its features.

  • Nearby WiFi Devices / Location permissions (required by Android to scan nearby WiFi networks)
  • Network access
  • Network state access
  • WiFi state access
  • Storage-related permissions where supported by your Android version

Permissions are only used to enable the features you choose to use.

Internet Speed Test

When you perform a speed test, the App connects to remote test servers to measure your internet connection performance.

The measured download speed, upload speed, latency, and related results are displayed only for your convenience. These results are not stored on our servers.

Storage & Cleanup

The Storage and Cleanup features analyze information available on your device, such as cache size, temporary files, residual files, and storage usage.

This analysis is performed locally on your device. We do not upload your storage information or personal files.

Analytics & Crash Reports

To improve app quality and stability, anonymous diagnostic information may be collected through trusted third-party services.

This information may include:

  • Device model
  • Android version
  • App version
  • Crash logs
  • Anonymous performance statistics

This information is used only to improve the App and does not include your WiFi passwords, browsing history, personal files, or other sensitive content.

Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services that provide analytics, crash reporting, advertising, or platform functionality.

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics

Advertisements

The App may display advertisements provided by Google AdMob or other advertising partners.

Advertising providers may collect advertising identifiers or other device identifiers according to their own privacy policies for ad delivery and measurement.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information processed by the App.

Because most information is processed locally on your device, protecting your device with a screen lock or biometric authentication is recommended.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.

Links to Third-Party Services

The App may contain links to third-party websites or services.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those external services and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect improvements to the App or changes in legal requirements.

The latest version will always be published on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the support page provided in the app store listing.
