Privacy Policy

Dog Translator: Pet Sounds

Effective date: July 21, 2026

Dog Translator: Pet Sounds ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected, used, and protected when you use the App.

By downloading or using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information You Provide

Some features allow you to create and manage information related to your pets. Depending on how you use the App, you may voluntarily provide:

  • Pet names
  • Pet breeds
  • Birthdays
  • Pet photos
  • Vaccination records
  • Care reminders
  • Personal notes about your pets

This information is stored locally on your device unless a future feature explicitly states that cloud synchronization is available.

Voice Recordings

The App may request microphone access to record your voice or your pet's sounds for entertainment features such as simulated pet translation.

Voice recordings are processed only to provide the selected feature and are not uploaded to our servers.

Photos

If you choose to add pet photos to your pet profile, the App will request access to your device's photo library or camera where supported.

Photos remain on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.

Pet Translation

The translation features are intended solely for entertainment purposes.

The App does not scientifically translate or interpret animal communication, behavior, emotions, or intentions.

Local Data Storage

Pet profiles, vaccination history, reminders, notes, and other information created within the App are primarily stored locally on your device.

If you uninstall the App or reset your device, this information may be permanently lost unless you have created your own backup.

Analytics & Crash Reports

To improve the reliability and performance of the App, anonymous diagnostic information may be collected through trusted third-party services.

This information may include:

  • Device model
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Crash reports
  • Anonymous usage statistics

These reports do not contain your pet profiles, recordings, notes, or photos.

Third-Party Services

The App may integrate trusted third-party services for analytics, crash reporting, advertising, or platform functionality.

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics

Advertisements

The free version of the App may display advertisements provided by third-party advertising partners such as Google AdMob.

Advertising providers may collect advertising identifiers or similar device identifiers in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Permissions

Depending on the features you use, the App may request permissions such as:

  • Microphone (voice recording)
  • Photos / Media access (pet profile images)
  • Camera (optional pet photos)
  • Notifications (vaccination and care reminders)
  • Internet access (ads, analytics, app updates)

Permissions are requested only when required for specific features.

Children's Privacy

The App is not specifically directed toward children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.

Data Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect information processed by the App.

Because most user-created content is stored locally, we recommend protecting your device using a screen lock or biometric authentication.

Your Choices

You may stop using the App at any time by uninstalling it.

Because your pet information is primarily stored locally, uninstalling the App may permanently remove your data unless it has been backed up separately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes to the App or applicable laws.

The latest version will always be available on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the support page listed in the app store.
